O Mnie

Dowiedz się O Mnie więcej


Pigmentacją oraz zdobieniem ciała interesowałam się już od wielu lat. Zanim odnalazłam pasję do makijażu permanentnego, zagłębiałam się w sekrety tatuażu. Dziś doceniam wiedzę, którą udało mi się zdobyć. W zawodzie linergistki bardzo ważne jest, aby znać różnicę pomiędzy makijażem permanentnym, a tatuażem. To właśnie subtelna pigmentacja i naturalne efekty były dla mnie od samego początku największą inspiracją i chęcią podjęcia zawodu linergistki. Makijaż sam w sobie nigdy nie skradł mojego serca. Oczywiście sama na co dzień maluję się, śledzę branżę kosmetyczną z ogromną uwagą i zainteresowaniem, ale jednocześnie jestem zdania, iż mocne makijaże nie podkreślają naszej urody. Jako osoba, która ceni indywidualny urok każdej kobiety, nie lubię przerysowanych efektów makijażu - jak również pigmentacji. Każda z nas ma w sobie ogromne pokłady piękna. Czasem wystarczy w umiejętny i subtelny sposób podkreślić naszą urodę, by wydobyć jej naturalny blask.

W pracy jestem bardzo wymagająca i sumienna. Wiem, że ciężka i systematyczna praca oraz chęć rozwoju są kluczem do sukcesu. Dążenie do perfekcji, dbanie o najmniejszy szczegół, budowanie dobrych relacji z klientem w połączeniu z prawdziwa miłością i pasją do tego co się robi są czynnikami, które są konieczne do bycia profesjonalistą w branży pmu. Z chęcią będę dzielić się z wami swoimi osiągnięciami.

Rules

1. By making an appointment for the procedure you accept in full the rules and regulations below.

2. If you want to book an appointment, please contact me through my fanpage on Facebook, or send me an e-mail.

3. When making an appointment for permanent make-up it is required to make an advance payment of £ 50.

Clients should make the advance payment within 3 days of making the appointment. If the payment is not made, the appointment is automatically cancelled and the date booked becomes available for other clients.

4. If the procedure is carried out, its price will be reduced by the amount of the advance payment.

5. The advance payment may be made by transfer to my bank account – Monika Stanko 20-43-04 93847896. In the transfer title please write the date of the procedure and the client’s name and surname.

6. The client has the right to change the date of the appointment 2 days before the planned appointment at the latest. If the client cancels the appointment at the last minute, that is on the same day, then, when booking another visit the client needs to make another advance payment. I recognise, however, extraordinary situations and random incidents (it must be proved that a given situation occurred).

7. If the appointment is cancelled later than 2 days in advance, the advance payment is lost.

8. If the client is more than 30 minutes late for the procedure, it should be rescheduled for a different date and another advance payment should be made to make a new appointment. 9. When making an appointment the client should make sure there are no contraindications against the procedure. A list of contraindications can be found on my website. If you are not sure as to certain contraindications – please write to me. I will be happy to answer your questions. 10. I recommend that you check the material available on my website before the procedure. You will find there, among others, a list of potential undesirable effects and recommendations on how to prepare for the procedure. It is very important that you know what the procedure is about, how it is carried out and what effects can be achieved. In case of additionally questions, please send me a message. 11. Clients who have had permanent make-up of their eyebrows done before (even slightly visible) are obliged to inform me about it and send me a current photograph to my e-mail address before making an appointment for the procedure. It is sometimes required to precede the permanent make-up procedure with laser removal. 12. We will select the method of pigmentation, shape and pigment colour together depending on mutual expectations, the client’s skin type and looks. Before the permanent make-up procedure I prepare a preliminary drawing which allows for visualisation of the final effect. 13. I reserve the right to refuse to provide the service if the client’s requirements are at variance with my sense of aesthetics. Photos of my works are available in social media: on my fanpage on Facebook and Instagram. Please see before you make an appointment for the procedure. 14. If the client does not accept the proposed shape, method and colour of pigment, and decides against pigmentation during the appointment – the advance payment will not be returned. 15. Supplementary pigmentation which is included in the price of the procedure is performed within 5 weeks to 3 months after the first pigmentation. 16. If the client fails to attend the appointment or cancel it 4 days before the planned procedure, another correction is paid – the price of such a service is £ 40, paid in advance. 17. Permanent make-up changes its intensity in the months following the procedure, which is why it is recommended to renew it after a year. The price of such a service is 50% of the current price up to 12 months from the first procedure, and up to 18 months it is a cost of 80% of the current price. 18. If you later decide that you need additional pigmentation to obtain more intense effects, the price of such a service is £ 40. Additional pigmentation is performed 3 months after the last procedure at the latest. The advance payment to be made when booking an appointment for such a procedure is £ 20. 19. If you really want to have permanent make-up done in an express mode, the price of such a service is 30% higher. 20.
